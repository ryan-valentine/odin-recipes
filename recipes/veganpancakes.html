Vegan Pancakes

Description

This is a delicious pancake recipe that I used to make Sasha almost every Sunday. Lately I've not been making it quite so often, but perhaps this weekend!

Either way, I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 2 TBSP Coconut Sugar
  • 1 TBSP Baking Powder
  • 1/2 TSP Salt
  • 1 Cup Almond Milk
  • 1 TBSP Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1 TBSP Almond Extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine the flour, coconut sucar, baking powder and salt. You can sift these together or mix thoroughly with a whisk.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the almond milk and apple cider vinegar. Let sit 5 minutes.
  3. Add the vanilla extract to the almond milk and apple cider vinegar concoction.
  4. Gently mix the wet ingredients into the dry. Do not overmnix or the pancakes will be tough. The final mixture may be slightly lumpy.
  5. Cook like pancakes on a sillet over medium heat!

If you really want to make your pancakes great, add walnuts or some shit, I don't care!
