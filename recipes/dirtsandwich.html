Dirt Sandwich

Description

Man oh man do I love dirt sandwiches. I think the best part is the way the dirt really grinds your teeth down to nubs. The crunchy grinding feeling really gets my goat.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • 1 Cup of Dirt
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • 1 Glass Whole Milk

Steps

  1. Lay our pieces of bread out on a flat surface
  2. Carefully spoon dirt onto one piece of bread to desired thickness. Go as heavy or light as you like!
  3. Put lettuce in garbage.
  4. Put onion on belt.
  5. Put the last piece of bread on top of the dirt. Dirt sandwich!
