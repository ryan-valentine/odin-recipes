Dirt Sandwich Two

Description

Look, I don't really want to make another recipe. Here's the dirt sandwich one again.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • 1 Cup of Dirt
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • 1 Glass Whole Milk

Steps

  1. Lay our pieces of bread out on a flat surface
  2. Carefully spoon dirt onto one piece of bread to desired thickness. Go as heavy or light as you like!
  3. Put lettuce in garbage.
  4. Put onion on belt.
  5. Put the last piece of bread on top of the dirt. Dirt sandwich!

Shove it down your gullet!
